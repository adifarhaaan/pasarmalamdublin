About

Welcome to Pasar Malam Dublin, a platform dedicated to connecting the Malaysian community in Dublin and providing a virtual space to showcase and sell delicious homemade food. We believe that food is a universal language that brings people together, and we want to keep this tradition alive even in a foreign land.
